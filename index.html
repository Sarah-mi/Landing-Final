My services

Postpartum Recovery Support

Tailored emotional support and physical comfort to address your unique needs. I'll care for your baby, allowing you to rest or take a well-deserved break.

Newborn Care Education

Guiding you through essential tasks such as bathing, diapering, dressing, swaddling, and soothing techniques. Gain confidence in caring for your newborn with personalized assistance.

Infant Feeding Assistance

Whether nursing or bottle-feeding, I provide support with optimal positions for comfort and ensure your baby receives the nutrition they need.

Sleep Guidance

Empowering you with insights into newborn sleep patterns, sleep hygiene, and safe sleep practices. Establish realistic expectations and receive tips for maximizing rest.

Light Meal Preparation

Engaging in light meal preparation and crafting healthy snacks to keep your body nourished and energy levels up. Full meal preparation services are also available upon request.

Light Housekeeping

Taking care of laundry, loading/unloading the dishwasher, tidying up, and organizing. Create a serene environment where you can focus on your baby without the distraction of a messy space.
